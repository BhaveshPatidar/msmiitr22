About Branch

If a man's wit be wandering, let him study mathematics.

Francis Bacon

Applied Mathematics is a combination of mathematical science and specilized knowledge. The term is self-explanatory, apply mathematics wherever you can.

The branch is highly flexible, contradictory to your initial thoughts.
Do you want to be a pro-coder, or maybe perform a ground-breaking research, maybe finance is your field of interest or like most of the people, are yet to find your interest? Let me assure you, this branch will open paths in all directions for you.

Mark my words, you will surely enjoy your 5 years of Applied Mathematics @ IITR.
